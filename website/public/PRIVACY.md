# Privacy Policy for 2nd Half Faith

**Last updated:** February 5, 2025

## Overview

2nd Half Faith is a personal content creation tool used to publish faith-based videos to TikTok. This privacy policy explains how we handle data.

## Data Collection

This application:
- Does **not** collect personal data from viewers
- Does **not** track users
- Does **not** use cookies or analytics
- Does **not** share data with third parties

## TikTok Integration

This app uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish videos to the @2ndhalf_faith TikTok account. We access only:
- Permission to upload videos to our own account
- Basic account info required for authentication

No viewer data, comments, or follower information is collected or stored.

## Data Storage

Content (videos, audio, text) is stored locally and on TikTok's platform after publishing. No user data is transmitted to external servers.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the account owner through TikTok @2ndhalf_faith.

## Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
